Features




The Goodness in Your Cup

A simple choice - like which coffee you choose - can make a difference for family farmers around the world. So have a cup with us and help make a difference.

Fair Trade Certified organic double certification helps you make a great cup of coffee and make a difference. Fair Trade Certified guarantees farmers a fair price for their harvest, allowing them to invest in their crops, their communities, and their future. Certified organic farming keeps chemicals out of the soil and water, creating coffee grown in harmony with the indigenous habitat. Feel confident that your purchase of any delicious Newman's Own organic coffee is benefiting others.

Great Tasting Coffee That Happens to Be Organic

We find that people really want to know how the ingredients are grown and processed on the way to their grocer's shelf. When people see how much organic foods have improved since the early days of packaged 'health' foods, we hope they'll try other organic food products.

Newman's Own Foundation continues Paul Newman's commitment to donate all royalties and its after tax profits from this product for charitable purposes. Paul Newman and Newman's Own Foundation have given over $400 Million to thousands of charities since 1982.

Choose Fair Trade

Fair Trade certification gives farmers a fair price for their beans with a guaranteed minimum, which means they can invest in their crops, their communities, and their future. Fair Trade also creates some of the best coffee in the world, because farmers can afford to follow time - honored traditions that produce the finest beans.

With a Fair Trade price for their coffee, farmers can afford better healthcare, food and housing. Families stay together, and kids can stay in school. When farmers can afford to take good care of themselves and their land, you get a better cup of coffee.

VARIETY DESCRIPTION: Bold yet refined. Strong, yet smooth. A study in contradiction or a reflection of the man for whom it was created?  Maybe both.  This inspired blend of medium-roasted Central American and darker-roasted Indonesian coffees is rich, robust, and satisfying. Made with 100% organic Arabica coffee.

ABOUT NEWMAN'S OWN ORGANICS:
GREAT TASTING COFFEE THAT HAPPENS TO BE ORGANIC. SOME RELATIONSHIPS ARE MEANT TO HAPPEN. It seems only natural that two companies with a shared vision of a better world would unite to bring you exceptional coffee and the chance to make a difference. Fair Trade organic coffee gives you great taste and the chance to make a real difference in the lives of coffee farmers around the world. Because farmers get a fair price for their crop, they are better able to take care of their land, their families, and themselves.

INGREDIENTS: ORGANIC ARABICA COFFEE
