5.0 out of 5 starsFINALLY! Bold and Beautiful!
ByB &amp; G Rodriguezon November 9, 2014
Flavor: Italian Roast Coffee|Size: 96 Count|Verified Purchase
These were for my husband and here is his review on the Barista Prima Coffeehouse Dark Roast Extra Bold K-Cups.

YES!!! I finally found a k-cup that is bold on the largest brew setting AND reasonably priced. We had purchased a Keurig back in 2007 and have loved it ever since. Back then, there were only two brewing cup sizes: Small and Large. We always brewed on the large and while my favorite k-cup was Tully's Italian Roast, we often got Newman's Extra Bold because it was readily available at Sam's Club. BUT, it was not "extra" bold, maybe extra medium.

In the last few years we started using a fill your own, reusable k-cups and would buy different bold roasts. Most were very good but the reusable k-cup thing is "kind of" a pain. Not terrible but certainly not as convenient as the disposable ones.

Anyway, our trusty old Keurig finally bit the dust a few months ago and so I got one of the newer models. This one has three brew settings: Small, Medium and Large. But the Medium brew size on this new one was the size of the large on our old one. So the new Large added even more ounces to the cup which SOUNDS glorious BUT...what was bold once before, was now just "Meh". :(

Some interesting notes regarding our new machine: While it was much more quiet than the old one, it didn't not handle the reusable cups well at all. It seemed to struggle to push the water through the cup. I suspect that part of the reason the new machines were more quiet (which was a welcome feature), was a reduction in motor size/power which is why it wasn't pushing through the coffee in our reusable cups.

So, we went back to the disposable ones and my quest to find a bold cup on the large setting began. I tried many "Bold" and dark roast cups and was getting unsatisfying cups of coffee for my taste. And then I found these little gems. I can brew these on the large cup size and they are BOLD, BEAUTIFUL and DELICIOUS!

At night, I sometimes smile when I think about that great cup of coffee waiting for me in the morning. If you like dark roasts and a good bold cup. I would highly recommend these!
